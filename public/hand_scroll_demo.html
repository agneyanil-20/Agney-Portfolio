HOW THE ENGINE TRANSLATES MOVEMENT

Behind the scenes, we load Google's @mediapipe/tasks-vision libraries from JSDelivr CDN. Rather than tracking random pixels, MediaPipe tracks 21 individual 3D hand joints.

Our engine measures the normalized delta vector of your wrist joint (Landmark 0) and finger tips. By storing a continuous window of coordinate packets, we extract the instantaneous vertical hand velocity, bypassing micro-jitters through low-pass exponential filters.

// Math Logic Tweak Window
const deltaY = currentY - previousY;
